How to get there

directions_car

By Private Car

Take the M4 Highway toward the Northern Valley. Take exit 88 labeled "Forest Array Preserve." Follow the winding scenic route for 12km. You will see a large stone entrance gate with the Greenwood Estate logo. Free valet parking is available for all guests.

train

Public Transport

Board the North-Western Line from Central Station to "Pine Crest Junction." From there, we offer a complimentary eco-shuttle service that runs every hour from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Please book your shuttle slot at least 24 hours in advance via our concierge.
